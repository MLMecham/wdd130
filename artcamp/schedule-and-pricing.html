Let's Review the Schedule

Join us for an exciting week of creativity and fun at our art camp, taking place from June 10th to the 14th in American Fork, conveniently located near Costco. The exact location will be disclosed to those who register, ensuring privacy and security for all participants.

To ensure the best experience for our young artists, we've organized two separate age groups: ages 3-5 and 6-10.

For our younger artists (ages 3-5), our sessions will run from 9:30 to 11:00 AM. We'll focus on exploring colors and shapes, drawing inspiration from beloved storybooks. Through engaging activities in painting, drawing, and printmaking, we'll delve into the vibrant world of the color wheel and much more!

For our older group (ages 6-10), sessions will be held from 11:15 AM to 12:45 PM. We'll sharpen our drawing and shading skills with guided art lessons, emphasizing how shapes serve as the foundation for captivating artwork. From learning painting techniques to bringing favorite characters to life on paper, we'll embark on a journey of artistic discovery through paints, printmaking, and beyond.

At the end of our enriching week, we'll celebrate the creativity and hard work of our young artists with a final art show on Friday. Families and friends are invited to join us as we showcase the masterpieces created throughout the week.

Pricing!

The cost for the entire week is $50, or participants may opt for a daily rate of just $10 for those who can only attend certain days. For flexibility, please reach out to us if you're unable to join us for all five days, and we'll adjust accordingly.
